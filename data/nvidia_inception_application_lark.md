**3. Locality is hierarchical.** Eidolon and nous (the 6-dim hub organs) propagate curvature changes globally when perturbed. Non-hub organs (4–5 dims) produce strictly local effects. The system has structural leverage points.

**4. R and couplings are independent controls.** Scalar curvature R = −n(n−1)(n+2)/8 exactly — confirmed across all growth steps. R tracks *dimensionality*. The couplings encode *geometry*. The system can independently control its scale and its shape.

All four findings are numerically confirmed and registered in NOEMA.
